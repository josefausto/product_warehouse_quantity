Available product's quantity per warehouse

  * To empower the user by providing the decision making knowledge is one of the key areas to focus while implementing ERP.
  * An effort towards providing analysed information mining through the already existed data.
  * Here is the Odoo module for multi warehouse implementation.
  * This module helps to calculate and show real time total quantity of the product available in each of the warehouse.
  * The total cumulative quantity will be calculated considering not only stock location of the warehousem but also all the sub locations of each of the stock location of all warehouses.

Warehouse structure

As an example, in the multi warehouse structure, each of the warehouse has one stock location. That stock location of the warehouse may have various child location.

Products in various locations

As an example, the different number of quantity of 'Apple In-Ear Headphones' product is there in such locations as shown here.

Product Form View

The total quantity of the product with corresponding warehouses information are displayed in the Product Form view.

Product Kanban View

The total quantity of the product with corresponding warehouses information are displayed in the Product Kanban view.

Access Control

Assign 'Show product quantity per warehouse' group to the user to which you want them to show number of available quantity of the product per warehouse.
